Practices & Protocols

COVID-19 Practices & Protocols

Flexible closing options for every party to the transaction — remote, mobile notary, or in person.

Sellers

Options for closing — remote or in person.

We've adapted the closing experience so sellers can choose the path that works best. Whether you prefer to finish from home or come to our office, we keep the process smooth and safe.

For Sellers

E-Close

Sellers in Washington, DC, Virginia, and Maryland can close remotely using Notarize.com with a $99.00 fee. Strongly encouraged.

In-Office

Come to our Capitol Hill office to sign in person. Documents are delivered as searchable PDFs through a secure portal ahead of closing.

Buyers & Refinance Borrowers

For Buyers / Refinance Borrowers

Three flexible paths to closing, depending on how your purchase is structured and where you'd like to sign.

In-Office

Most team members work remotely, with office staff on hand for in-person closings. Sign in our Capitol Hill office when it's the right fit for your transaction.

From Home

Mobile notary services available ($160.00 fee) since lenders don't permit e-notarization.

Cash Buyers, E-Close

Cash purchasers may e-close in all three jurisdictions without lender documents.

Agents & Lenders

For Real Estate Agents & Lenders

Agents and loan officers are welcome at settlements when client preference has been verified. For remote closings, e-closing documents are reviewed via conference call, and clients then notarize through Notarize.com.

Funds

EMD & Cash to Close

Wire transfers are strongly encouraged.
